Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli's success story is a testament to his unparalleled dedication, remarkable work ethic, and exceptional batting prowess, propelling him to become one of cricket's modern legends. New Delhi: Virat Kohli, an iconic figure in international cricket, was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India

The Indian skipper has dominated the cricketing world with his aggressive style of play, 
            consistent performances, and unmatched leadership skills. 
            He is often referred to as the "King" or "King Kohli" by his fans and supporters due to 
            his achievements and contributions to the game.
